The Robots Are Taking Over?!?!?!

Oh wait...that's not for another 7 years. Robots currently lack human creativity, intuition, and emotional intelligence. They are programmed to follow rules and algorithms, and can't deviate from their instructions. Robots also require a lot of resources and human intervention to function, including power, maintenance, and programming.

Artificial intelligence (AI)

AI will continue to advance in various sectors, including healthcare, finance, and transportation. AI-powered systems can perform tasks that are usually associated with human cognitive functions.

Virtual reality (VR) and augmented reality (AR)

AR and VR technologies will become more mainstream, allowing consumers to try products virtually, explore virtual stores, and attend virtual events.

Internet of Things (IoT)

More devices will connect to the internet, enabling automation and communication across sectors like healthcare, agriculture, and home automation.

Advanced robotics

Robotics technology will continue to evolve, creating machines that can perform complex tasks autonomously or with minimal human oversight.
